Place your video files here with these exact names:

  video-1.mp4              (Services page video player)
  video-2.mp4              (Services page video player)
  back-pain-relief.mp4     (About page exercise section)

How to get them from Facebook:
1. Open Dr. Noman Khan's Facebook page in a browser.
2. Open the video post you want to use.
3. Click the three-dot menu (•••) on the post -> "Download" (if available),
   or use a trusted Facebook video downloader tool by pasting the post's
   public URL.
4. Save the downloaded file into this folder (public/videos/) and rename
   it to match one of the exact filenames above.

Recommended format: .mp4 (H.264), under ~30MB each for fast loading.
If your files are large, compress them first (e.g. with HandBrake or
ffmpeg: ffmpeg -i input.mp4 -vcodec libx264 -crf 28 video-1.mp4

Once the files are in this folder, the matching video player picks them
up automatically -- no code changes needed:
  /videos/video-1.mp4, /videos/video-2.mp4
    -> Services page (src/components/VideoShowcase.jsx)
  /videos/back-pain-relief.mp4
    -> About page exercise section (src/pages/AboutPage.jsx)
