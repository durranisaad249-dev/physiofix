How to get them from Facebook:
1. Open Dr. Noman Khan's Facebook page in a browser.
2. Open the video post you want to use.
3. Click the three-dot menu (•••) on the post -> "Download" (if available),
   or use a trusted Facebook video downloader tool by pasting the post's
   public URL.
4. Save the downloaded file into this folder (public/videos/) and rename
   it to video-1.mp4 (and the second one to video-2.mp4).

Recommended format: .mp4 (H.264), under ~30MB each for fast loading.
If your files are large, compress them first (e.g. with HandBrake or
ffmpeg: ffmpeg -i input.mp4 -vcodec libx264 -crf 28 video-1.mp4

Once both files are in this folder, the video player on the Services page
(src/components/VideoShowcase.jsx) will automatically pick them up at
/videos/video-1.mp4 and /videos/video-2.mp4 -- no code changes needed.
